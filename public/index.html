Keep your neighborhood clean.

OpenWaste.

Litter is undoubtedly a huge problem in society today. Whether it be at the beach or in your own neighborhood, there’s no shortage of littered trash that you’ve seen on the floor. Oftentimes, even the good-willed people are quick to turn a blind eye to this litter simply due to the inconvenience of picking it up and finding a rare public trash can to throw it in.
OpenWaste solves this problem and reduces litter by allowing people to open their trash cans to their community. Now, when someone sees a piece of trash on the ground in their neighborhood, all they have to do is go to the nearest residency, most likely very close by, to dispose of the trash.
